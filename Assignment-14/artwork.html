Laurie Andersons Works

This work is called the Handphone Table. How this piece works is that the sound the vibrates on the table is traveled by bone conduction since the elbows do not have thick flesh. Her inspiration on this piece was when she heard the sound of the hummin typewritter transferred through the wooden table via the contact points her elbows. This is one of her few popular sound sculpture pieces.

Zimouns Works

Unnamed sculpture (2014). This piece has numerous cardboard boxes with motors attached with wires, and small balls. When these play all together creates a rhythm and create tension between orderly patterns of modernism and the chaotic forces of life.

Unmaned sculpture (2016). This sculpture has hundreds of brown paper bags and motors inside each one of them. The wire and ball attached to the motor are at a slow speed and sounds like crumpling paper.
