About Starset

This Ohio rock band Starset emerged in the mid-2010s, described as "cinematic rock" and devloping a thematic narrative. Majority of Starsets songs are science ficiton-inspired themes. Starsets commitment to their unique concept has set them apart in the modern rock scene. With their music and narrative have struck a chord with fans who share their interests in science, technology, and science fiction, making the band integral to a community united by a shared passion for exploration, knowledge, and music.
