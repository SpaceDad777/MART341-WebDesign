Danger. Darkness. Dwarves

Deep Rock Galactic is a first-person shooter where the players play as dwarves, space miners employed by Deep Rock Galactic. In teams up to four players explore, mine resources, complete objectives, and face a variety of alien creatures. The dynamic terrain deformation system allows players to dig and drill, creating their own paths through the challenging environments. The main goal involves extracting valuuable minerals while surviving encounters with hostile creatures.

Class

Having a well-blanced team is crucial to succeed in Deep Rock Galactic. A well blaanced team that leverages the strengths of each class is bettwe equipped to handle the challenge of the underground environment. These are the four main classes:


Gunner

The gunner specializes in heavy firepower, wielding a minigun and a powerful revolver. Gunner provides sustaintial firepower to deal with swarms of hostile creatures. The gunner also offeres consistent heavy damage and carries more ammunition than the other classes.


Driller

Driller is equipped with powerful digging and tunneling tools, such as a flamethrower and drills. They excel at creating pathways through solid rock and provide shortcut or tunnels into other areas for the team. The Driller opens new routes and assist in resource extraction. THeir ability to shape the environment is vital for the team's exploration and success in various mission objectives.


Engineer

The engineer is engi-here. They excel at providing defensive support and creating platforms to assist with difficult terrain. Engineers bring deployable turrents, grenade launchers, and platform guns. They play a critical role in fortifying positions, and setting up defenses gives them the ability to control the battlefield.


Scout

Scouts are the reconnaissance specialists armed with a pwerfuul shotgun and a flare gun. They have a grappling hook for quick traversal and can provide valuable information about the environment. Scouts are essential for exploration and anvigation. Their mobility allows them to scout ahead, locate key objectives, and illuminate dark areas with flares. Their ability to reach high or distant locations make them important for uncovering resources and ensuring the teams overall awareness.
